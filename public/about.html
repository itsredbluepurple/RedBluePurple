The idea

One glance tells you if a company is worth your time.

Describe what you want in one prompt. After that, every company page you open gets a verdict — scored against your criteria, not someone else's idea of a good job.

How it works

Set it once. Then just browse.

01

Write your criteria

On the Settings page, describe what you want in one prompt — companies worth your time and the dealbreakers to skip. Change it whenever you like.

02

Open any page

Browse job boards and company sites the way you already do. No copy-paste, no spreadsheet, no extra tab.

03

Read the verdict

Red Blue Purple judges every listing against your prompt and pins a verdict — aligned, mixed, flagged, or neutral — so you decide in a second, not an afternoon.
